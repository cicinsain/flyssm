Detailed Description

Annealing parameters that are not specific to the Lam algorithm.

In general the parameters should be used in moves.c or fly_sa.c but not in lsa.c. In the data file, the members of the struct labeled RO are read from the $annealing_input section. They are used for initial conditions of the annealer and do not change during a run. Members labeled OUT are written to the $annealing_output section upon completion of a run.
